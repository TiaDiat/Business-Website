Health & Safety

We are committed in keeping the safety & well being of all clients and staff. We set high standards in sterilization and sanitation of all implements. We use Hospital Grade disinfectants & sterile equipments. All nonsterile implements such as nail buffers, files, foot pumices, & wax applicators are disposed after each use.
